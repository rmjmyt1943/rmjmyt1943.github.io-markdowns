## Why digital displays?
Digital displays allow flexible, high-impact presentation of media content for events, signage, retail, museums, and broadcast.

## Common use cases
- Broadcast control rooms and confidence monitors
- Event staging and LED video walls
- Digital signage and wayfinding
- Interactive kiosks and touch exhibits

## Key specs to consider
- Resolution (PPI / pixel pitch for LED)
- Brightness (nits) for ambient light
- Viewing angle and color accuracy
- Refresh rate and input lag for video or interactive use
